Who We Are


We are your committed partner to fuel your growth A tailored approach to value addition, technology adoption, and sustainability!

At Ster Impact, we believe in the power of women in agribusiness to transform communities. With a mission to empower women in production, processing, and distribution of local farm-based goods, we are committed to providing resources, support, and education to cultivate entrepreneurship in the agricultural sector.



Investment Focus

Value Addition, Technology, and Sustainability in Agribusiness


Value addition

Supporting businesses that process and package agricultural products, increasing their market value and creating jobs.

Technology Adoption

Investing in innovative technologies that improve agricultural productivity, efficiency, and resilience to climate change.

Sustainability

Promoting environmentally sound agricultural practices that conserve natural resources and minimize environmental impact.




Core Values


Empowerment

We are committed to empowering women by providing the tools and knowledge to succeed in agribusiness.

Innovation

We believe in the power of innovative solutions to address challenges and create growth opportunities in agribusiness.

Community

Building a strong community is at the heart of everything we do. Together, we can make a lasting impact.
