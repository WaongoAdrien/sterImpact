Grow Star Entrepreneurs, Lift Communities!


At Ster Impact, we are your trusted growth partner — committed to transforming Africa’s agribusiness, one entrepreneur at a time.

We empower agri-SMEs with smart investment, innovation, and value creation — driving inclusive growth, strong returns for investors, and resilience for our planet.

OUR APPROACH

We provide tailored financing and practical support to help you scale your business and deepen your impact. For investors, we are your gateway to Africa’s untapped potential. We identify and develop investment opportunities that deliver solid financial returns while driving real, tangible impact. We are a local team driven by global standards. We bring our strong work ethic, ability to adapt best practices to local context and commitment to excellence to unlock the next generation of Africa’s entrepreneurs.

Join us, and be part of our journey!

Mission

Transform Africa’s agribusiness by accelerating agro-processing, reversing the export of raw products to capture value, create jobs and reshape the local economy. We drive capital to an under-resourced and high-impact sector.


Our vision

Our vision is an Africa where agribusinesses capture local value and power inclusive prosperity, resilient communities, and sustainable growth for generations to come.
