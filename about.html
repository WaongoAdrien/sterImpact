About US

A Gender-Lens Investing Philosophy
We are a gender lens investing initiative tailored to scale up agribusinesses and enable women to thrive. We are focusing on value addition, technology adoption and sustainability. Agribusiness in Africa represents a tremendous opportunity but very often there is limited value addition to raw materials i.e. little to no transformation. There is a funding gap, especially for women when they actually make up to 80% of the workforce. Technology adoption and sustainability can significantly increase productivity and revenues. Ster Impact Fund mission is to leverage our background in Industrial Engineering and Finance to provide customized financing and technical solutions including value chain optimization, business knowledge, ESG opportunities.

Economic Empowerment
Investing in women unleashes their economic potential, leading to increased household income and improved living standards.

Sustainable Development
Women are key drivers of sustainable agricultural practices, ensuring long-term food security and environmental stewardship.

Social Impact
Empowering women creates a ripple effect, strengthening communities and fostering gender equality across society.

Investment Focus in Agribusiness

Value Addition
Supporting businesses that process and package agricultural products, increasing their market value and creating jobs.

Technology Adoption
Investing in innovative technologies that improve agricultural productivity, efficiency, and resilience to climate change.

Sustainability
Promoting environmentally sound agricultural practices that conserve natural resources and minimize environmental impact.
