Africa’s Agribusiness, The Next Growth Frontier!

By 2030, the market will hit a trillion dollars, driven by a growing population of 2.5 billion by 2050. With 65% of the world’s uncultivated arable land, agro-processing boosts jobs, creating 2.8 more per role. SMEs, making up 99% of businesses and 60% of employment, are key to this growth.



Our Mission

♦︎ Innovate ♦︎ Accelerate economic profitability ♦︎ Lift communities ♦︎ Protect the environment

We aim to accelerate the sustainable growth and competitiveness of women-led agribusiness SMEs in Sub-Saharan Africa. Together, let’s explore a unique opportunity to invest in a high-growth, high-impact and sustainable sector.

Gender-Lens Investing Strategy

Women represents 60-80% of the agribusiness labor force, Investing in women in Africa drives economic growth, as women reinvest up to 90% of their income into their families and communities, improving education, health, and overall well-being.

Collaborate, work, and/or invest with us for positive and lasting impact on communities and strong financial returns.


What We Offer

Technical Assistance

We provide hands-on technical assistance to equip women with the skills and tools they need to thrive in agribusiness. From Knowledge to Impact – Our expert support helps women adopt innovative technologies, boost productivity, and build sustainable, high-value businesses.

Capacity Building

We provide women with the skills and tools needed to thrive in agriculture. Developing Talent, Creating Opportunities – Our capacity-building programs help women innovate, expand, and drive sustainable change.

Financing

We provide women with the financial support they need to grow and succeed. Investing in Women, Transforming Communities – Our financing empowers women to build thriving businesses and drive economic progress.

Curated Networks

Connecting Women, Creating Opportunities – Our curated network links women to mentors, markets, and resources for lasting success. Stronger Together, Growing Faster – We build a powerful community where women support, learn, and thrive together.
