“Why does Africa export raw cashew nuts and import processed cashew?”

This isn't unique to one country — cashew represents many products across the continent. Ster-Impact mission is to help reverse that conundrum. We take bold steps to transform Africa's agribusiness value chain. We do so in a sustainable way that benefits:

► Entrepreneurs
► Communities
► Partners and the planet.
