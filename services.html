For Entrepreneurs: How to Apply for Funding


♦︎ Review our investment criteria to ensure your business aligns with our focus areas.

♦︎ Prepare a comprehensive business plan outlining your business model, financial projections, and impact metrics.

♦︎ Submit your application through our online portal.

♦︎ Participate in our due diligence process, which may include interviews and site visits.
