Fair Trade Coffee Plantation

Fair Trade Coffee Plantation isn't just about coffee-it's about empowerment, sustainability, and ethical growth. This women-led initiative ensures fair wages, safe working conditions, and economic independence for women coffee growers.

Organic Vegetable Farm

Starting as a small family farm, this organic vegetable business flourished through sustainable farming and innovative techniques. With Ster Impact Fund's support, they expanded operations, improved irrigation, and increased yields while staying eco-friendly. Their growth created jobs for women and youth, strengthening the local economy. Proof that investing in women-led agribusiness drives lasting impact.
