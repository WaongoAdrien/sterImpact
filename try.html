Our Mission


Create Value

Our mission is to enhance agricultural value by supporting businesses that process and package products, creating jobs and boosting market potential.

Women as a vector


Woman led Development

Investing in women in Africa drives economic growth, as women reinvest up to 90% of their income into their families and communities, improving education, health, and overall well-being.

What We Offer

Technical Assistance

We provide hands-on technical assistance to equip women with the skills and tools they need to thrive in agribusiness. From Knowledge to Impact – Our expert support helps women adopt innovative technologies, boost productivity, and build sustainable, high-value businesses.

Capacity Building

We provide women with the skills and tools needed to thrive in agriculture. Developing Talent, Creating Opportunities – Our capacity-building programs help women innovate, expand, and drive sustainable change.

Financing

We provide women with the financial support they need to grow and succeed. Investing in Women, Transforming Communities – Our financing empowers women to build thriving businesses and drive economic progress.

Curated Networks

Connecting Women, Creating Opportunities – Our curated network links women to mentors, markets, and resources for lasting success. Stronger Together, Growing Faster – We build a powerful community where women support, learn, and thrive together.
